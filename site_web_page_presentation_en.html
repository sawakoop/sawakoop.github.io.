INTRODUCTION

Centres d'intérêt

Music :

Music holds a central place in my interests. It's a passion that drives me every day. Whether at home, on the go, or at work, I am constantly accompanied by my favorite music. My musical taste is rather eclectic, ranging from metal to piano, among various other genres. I find the instrumental aspect of a song crucial, as it can evoke a real enthusiasm in me for a track. Although I'm not always attentive to the lyrics, I take the time to appreciate them when some particularly touch me. Currently, my musical preferences lean towards Kpop, Pop, and Rap, with artists such as Stray Kids, IVE, aespa, Post Malone, and Damso. Among the tracks that have caught my attention recently are "MANIAC" by VIVIZ, "Straight Line" by Kep1er, and "The Killa (I Belong to You)" by TOMORROW X TOGETHER. I highly recommend checking out the EP "IVE SWITCH" by IVE, released this April 29th, as well as the album "5-STAR" by Stray Kids, released last June. These works are truly worth checking out.


Dance :

Dance represents one of my strongest interests, a passion rooted in my life since my early years. It's an intimate relationship that persists over time, where every step brings me a sense of well-being, like a recharge of energy. Dance allows me to escape, relax, and deeply connect with music, offering me a welcome respite from the daily whirlwind. Although I've never taken formal lessons, I consider myself proficient in this art. I don't have a particular dance style, enjoying both energetic movements and calmer ones alike. Recently, I've been engaged in learning choreographies to Kpop music, an experience that is both fun and stimulating. Among my favorite choreographies are "I AM" by IVE and "Teddy Bear" by STAYC, which I've mastered perfectly. Currently, I'm taking on the challenge of learning the choreography for "MAGO" by GFRIEND, a demanding yet captivating task. For me, dance and music form an inseparable duo, a harmonious combination that enriches my daily life.


Volleyball :

Volleyball holds a special place in my interests, being my sport of choice. While I may not master all the technical subtleties of this sport, every time I step onto the court, I feel an intense satisfaction in playing. Volleyball offers a unique combination of fun and camaraderie with teammates, while also providing a dose of competition, focus, and support during matches. This blend of elements allows me to stay both focused and relaxed. I discovered my passion for volleyball during my high school years, particularly in my senior year, during post-class sessions with friends and during physical education classes. Later, during my college years, I decided to take the plunge by joining a volleyball club, drawn to the atmosphere and team spirit of this sport. I am currently a member of my club for the second consecutive year, although I am currently taking a several-week break for health reasons.


Cooking :

Cooking is one of my hobbies, although not as deep-seated as my other interests, it is still important to me. I've been indulging in it for several years now. Initially focused on preparing savory dishes, I am now delving into the realm of pastry. I enjoy constantly experimenting with new recipes while seeking to perfect those I master best. Among my creations, my signature dish consists of fresh gnocchi accompanied by a delicious sauce made with cream, veal stock, mushrooms, and Parmesan cheese. It's a comforting recipe, perfect for autumn and winter evenings. Recently, I had the opportunity to discover a Cinnamon Rolls recipe on Instagram, and I must say it was one of the best pastries I've ever made. With the arrival of summer, I plan to explore new recipes and focus more on sweet creations.


Others :

I have several other passions deeply rooted within me. Although I'm not a avid reader, I am increasingly finding pleasure in immersing myself in reading. Currently, I am mainly focusing on manga, but I'm also trying to explore other literary genres, such as science fiction, and I intend to start the Dune saga. Regarding manga, my favorites include One Piece, Mob Psycho, and Death Note. Besides reading, design is another passion of mine. I am very meticulous and greatly appreciate the visual aspect of things. For example, I take pride in working on the design of this portfolio, seeking to give it a distinctive graphical touch. Like many people my age, I also enjoy video games. Even though I play them less these days, I still find pleasure in relaxing while playing games such as Overwatch 2, where I can focus, have fun, and strengthen teamwork, or The Legend Of Zelda: Tears of The Kingdom, which offers an immersive gaming experience where I can explore the world at my own pace. Lastly, hiking is one of my favorite activities. I particularly enjoy quiet, beautiful places close to nature, which allow me to recharge. Recently, I had the opportunity to go to Cantal with three friends, and this experience was one of the most refreshing and memorable of my life. I discovered charming places and I hope to have the opportunity to return someday.

About me

Who am I ?

My name is Baptiste Tivrier. I am currently a second-year student in the University of Technology Bachelor's degree program in Data Science at the IUT Paris Rives de Seine specializing in the "Visualization and Design of Decision-Making Tools Specialization" track. Passionate about computer science, mathematics, and data analysis, I chose this program for its ability to combine these fields. I consider this discipline as a fertile ground offering many opportunities for learning and professional development. During my academic journey, I had the opportunity to engage in a rewarding work-study experience. Since October 2023, I have been employed at the General Directorate of Administration and Public Service in Paris. This opportunity allows me to apply my theoretical knowledge to real-world situations while contributing to solving concrete problems with business data.


Later ?

In the future, after completing my University Bachelor's degree, my goal is to continue my studies by enrolling in a Master's program in Applied Mathematics or Statistics. This decision stems from my desire to continue exploring and deepening my knowledge in these areas, with the aim of pursuing a fulfilling and stimulating professional career.
